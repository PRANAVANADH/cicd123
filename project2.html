About Us

Welcome to **The Spice Route Bistro**, your passport to an authentic Asian culinary experience right here in **Hyderabad, Telangana**. We are passionate about bringing you the rich and diverse flavors from across the Asian continent, prepared with fresh, high-quality ingredients and traditional techniques. Our cozy ambiance makes us the perfect spot for a delightful family dinner, a romantic evening, or a casual lunch with friends.

Menu Highlights

  • Hakka Noodles - Stir-fried noodles with crisp vegetables and your choice of chicken or paneer.
  • Thai Green Curry - Aromatic coconut milk curry with bamboo shoots, bell peppers, and fresh herbs, served with jasmine rice.
  • Dim Sum Platter - A delightful assortment of steamed and pan-fried dumplings with various fillings.
  • Sichuan Chilli Chicken - Crispy chicken tossed in a fiery Sichuan sauce with dried chilies and cashews.
